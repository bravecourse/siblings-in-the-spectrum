Socialize

Interact With Others!

Sometimes it's hard to think of ourselves when there is a lot to do or worry about with our autistic siblings. It may sound selfish, but it's critical to be your own person as well.

Make sure you are:
  • Spending time with friends
  • Spending time with other family members
  • Having fun outside of the house
  • Talking to peers who also have autistic siblings
